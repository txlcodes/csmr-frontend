Our Mission

The Centre for Sustainability and Management Research (CSMR) was established in 2015 with a mission to advance knowledge in sustainable management practices through rigorous research, collaboration, and dissemination of findings to academic and professional communities worldwide.

We are dedicated to promoting innovative solutions to contemporary sustainability challenges through interdisciplinary research that bridges theory and practice.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized research institution that drives positive change in management practices and contributes to the advancement of sustainability science and policy.

We envision a future where sustainability principles are integrated into all aspects of organizational management and policy development, creating resilient and responsible systems that benefit both society and the environment.

CSMR Vision Image

Our Core Values

Academic Integrity

We are committed to the highest standards of research ethics, transparency, and intellectual honesty.

Sustainability

We promote research that contributes to environmental stewardship, social equity, and economic prosperity.

Innovation

We embrace creative approaches to research and encourage novel solutions to complex problems.

Collaboration

We foster partnerships across academic disciplines, institutions, and geographic boundaries.

Our History

2015

Foundation

CSMR was established as a research centre with initial funding from the Ministry of Education and private sector partners.

2017

First Publications

Launch of the Journal of Sustainable Management and Innovation & Sustainability Review.

2019

International Recognition

CSMR journals were indexed in major international databases, and the centre established partnerships with universities in Asia and Europe.

2021

Research Excellence Award

Received the National Research Excellence Award for contributions to sustainability research.

2024

Digital Transformation

Complete revamp of digital platforms and launch of enhanced online research repositories.
