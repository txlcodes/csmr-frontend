About the Journal

Management Studies Quarterly is a premier peer-reviewed journal that publishes high-quality research in all areas of management and organizational studies. Our journal serves as a bridge between academic research and practical management applications, contributing to the advancement of management knowledge and practice.

Scope and Focus Areas

  • Strategic management and organizational behavior
  • Human resource management and leadership
  • Operations and supply chain management
  • Marketing and consumer behavior
  • Finance and accounting
  • International business and cross-cultural management
  • Entrepreneurship and innovation
  • Corporate governance and ethics
