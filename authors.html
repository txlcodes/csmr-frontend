Publishing with CSMR

The Centre for Sustainability and Management Research (CSMR) welcomes high-quality research contributions that advance the fields of sustainable management, innovation, and environmental policy. By publishing with us, you'll join a community of researchers committed to addressing the complex challenges of sustainability in the 21st century.

Submission Guidelines Submit Manuscript

Publication Process

1

Submission

Authors submit manuscripts through our online submission system. Initial checks for scope, formatting, and plagiarism are conducted.

2

Peer Review

Manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review by at least two experts in the field. Reviewers evaluate content for originality, methodology, and relevance.

3

Revision

Authors address reviewer comments and resubmit the revised manuscript. Multiple rounds may be necessary.

4

Decision

The editor makes a final decision based on reviewer recommendations and the quality of revisions.

5

Production

Accepted manuscripts undergo copyediting, typesetting, and proofreading before final publication.

6

Publication

Final article is published online and in print, with DOI assignment and indexing in relevant databases.

Open Access Publishing

CSMR is committed to making research accessible to all. We offer various open access options across our journal portfolio:

  • Gold Open Access: Immediate open access to published articles, with a publication fee.
  • Green Open Access: Self-archiving in repositories after an embargo period.
  • Hybrid Journals: Both subscription and open access options available.

We support institutional agreements and waivers for researchers from low-income countries.

Open Access Policy

Publication Fee Structure

Journal Type Article Processing Charge (APC) Notes
Full Open Access Journals ₹20,000 / $250 Required for all accepted papers
Hybrid Journals (OA Option) ₹30,000 / $375 Optional for accepted papers
Subscription-Based Journals No charge Reader pays via subscription

Fee Waivers and Discounts

We offer complete or partial waivers to researchers from low and middle-income countries as defined by the World Bank. Discounts are also available for early-career researchers and authors from partner institutions.

To apply for a waiver or discount, please contact waivers@csmr.org before submission.
