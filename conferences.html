Academic Conferences & Events

CSMR organizes and sponsors a variety of conferences, symposia, and workshops that bring together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers from around the world. These events provide valuable opportunities for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaboration in the fields of sustainability and management research.

Become a Conference Host or Partner

CSMR welcomes collaboration with universities, research institutes, and organizations interested in hosting or co-organizing conferences and events related to sustainability and management research.

Partnering with CSMR offers several benefits, including:

  • Access to our network of researchers and experts
  • Promotional support through our channels
  • Assistance with conference organization and planning
  • Publication opportunities for conference proceedings
