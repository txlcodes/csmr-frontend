About the Journal

The Innovation & Sustainability Review is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research on sustainable innovation, green technologies, and responsible business practices. Our journal serves as a platform for researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to share insights on how innovation can drive sustainable development.

Scope and Focus Areas

  • Sustainable technology innovation
  • Green business models and circular economy
  • Renewable energy and clean technology
  • Sustainable supply chain management
  • Environmental policy and regulation
  • Social innovation and inclusive development
