Circular Economy Practices in the Indian Textile Industry: A Comparative Analysis

Vikram Singh, Meera Patel, Sanjay Gupta

This paper analyzes circular economy adoption in the Indian textile industry through a comparative case study of three major textile manufacturing clusters. Evaluating the Effectiveness of Climate Adaptation Policies in South Asian Urban Centers

Arjun Kumar, Neha Singh, Thomas Chen

This research evaluates climate adaptation policies implemented across 15 major urban centers in South Asia between 2015-2025.
