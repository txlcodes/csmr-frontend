Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a manuscript to one of your journals?

You can submit manuscripts through our online submission portal. Please visit our Submission Guidelines page for detailed instructions on the submission process.

What is the typical review timeline for submissions?

The initial review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. If accepted for peer review, the complete process may take 3-4 months depending on the journal and subject area.

How can I subscribe to your journals?

Institutional and individual subscriptions are available. Please contact our subscription department at subscriptions@csmr.org for rates and subscription options.

Does CSMR offer opportunities for research collaboration?

Yes, we actively seek research partnerships with academic institutions, industry, and government organizations. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@csmr.org to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
